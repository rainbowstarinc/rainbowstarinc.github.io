The Practice

How the Oracle works

I work with a sacred set of tools — cards, symbols, and the field between us — to bring through guidance that is specific to you, right now.

Readings can be integrated into a full healing session or offered as a standalone experience. They are held in sacred space, with full presence.

This work is best when you arrive with an open question — not seeking a fixed answer, but an expanded perspective.
