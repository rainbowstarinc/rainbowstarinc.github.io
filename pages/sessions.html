The Work

What happens in a session

Each session is unique, responsive, and alive. I work intuitively with whatever wants to be seen, moved, or released in your field. No two sessions are the same.

Sessions may include hands-on bodywork, sound and toning, crystal placement, color and light, oracle guidance, and conversation. They unfold at their own pace.

You may experience deep relaxation, emotional release, expanded states of awareness, or simply a profound sense of being held. All of it is welcome.
