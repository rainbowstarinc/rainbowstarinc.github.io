The Story

In my own words

RainbowStar emerged from years of walking the intersection of creative expression and healing arts — discovering that the body, the brush, and the cosmos are all speaking the same language.

I am a practitioner, a painter, and a student of the living world. My work is rooted in a deep reverence for the body's intelligence, the wisdom of plant medicines, and the organizing field of love that underlies all things.

Each painting I make is a transmission. Each session I hold is a collaboration with something larger. I show up as a conduit — and the healing, as always, belongs to you.
